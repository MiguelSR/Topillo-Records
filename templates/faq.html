FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  • Who are you?

    We're Topillo Records, a small label and distro from Spain.

    Send us a mail to blackhandzine@gmail.com for further information.

  • What are the shipping costs?

    It depends. Up to 500g (i.e. 4 or less CDs) is something like the following table. For bigger quantities just ask.

    Spain Europe Rest of the world
    Unregistered 2 € 6 € 9 €
    Registered 4 € 8 € 12 €

    We can also send 1 CD unregistered and without case for 1 € / 2 € / 3 € (Spain, Europe, Rest of World).

  • I run a label/distro, can I get some of your CDs?

    Sure, mate! We are interested in trading and we also have wholesale prices. Send a mail, please!
