Releases

These are our releases so far. You can listen to all of them with the embedded Bandcamp player, or listen to them in our Bandcamp page. Enjoy!

The Temple / Acolytes Of Moros - Strength For A New Dawn / The Bitter Wind (12'' EP)

Release date: May 2017

Brand new split of the doom metal bands The Temple and Acolytes of Moros. Both are among the best current bands in the traditional doom metal genre. The Temple, from Greece, plays Epic Doom metal the way it should be, solemn and evocative. Acolytes Of Moros, from Sweden, plays a dark doom metal that takes influences of many genres (including black metal!) but without losing the doom essence. Both bands deliver one long song. A true doomed masterpiece in a really beautiful vinyl. You can find a teaser at our Youtube channel.

Lucifer's Fall - Fuck You We're Lucifer's Fall (MCD)

Release date: September 2016

New MCD of these australian badass doomers. We're extremely pleased of releasing this stuff, as they are one of the best doom bands around. Motherfucking doom metal, powerful and heavy, only for freaks of doom and riff worshippers.

The Human Condition - Pathways (CD)

Release date: June 2016

Debut album of this english band. 50 minutes of epic, technical and sorrowful doom metal. With members and ex-members of Unsilence, The River and Misericorde.
